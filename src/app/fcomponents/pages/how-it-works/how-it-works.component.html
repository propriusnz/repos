How it Works
What we do
Learnspace is an online platform where learners from primary to university can find and take lessons with trained tutors. Our staff vets every application rigorously, and only the finest applicates are accepted, ensuring absolute quality for our learners.


Our Tutors
All Learnspace tutors are throughly vetted with police and reference checked.


Our Prices
Prices are set by tutors individually, and are shown on each profile. These prices are final, NO extra fees apply.


Payments
Payments are accepted through online transfer. Invoices will be sent for every session.


Where do lessons take place ?
Lessons may take place at any location within the tutor's service area (listed on the tutor's profile as location).
